# Instagram platform playbook (2026 research)

## Algorithm — what ranks Reels in 2026
- Top ranking signals: **watch time / early retention, likes-per-reach, sends-per-reach**. DM **shares are weighted 3–5× more than likes**. Optimize every reel to be *sendable* ("send this to your interview-prep buddy").
- Reels up to 3 min can be recommended to non-followers (Explore + Reels feed), but retention beats length: a 10s reel at 80% retention outperforms a 60s reel at 30%.
- ~50% of viewers drop in the first 3 seconds. 3-second hold >60% → 5–10× more reach than <40%. No slow intros, ever.
- Algorithm now **deprioritizes over-produced content**; raw/clear beats studio-polished. Establish 2–3 consistent content pillars so the algorithm can categorize the account.

## Hooks (first 3 seconds)
- On-screen text hook: ≤6–8 words, bold, high-contrast, centered.
- Pattern interrupts: contrarian claim, curiosity gap, unexpected visual motion (sudden zoom/pan).
- Hook formulas that fit this niche: "Why Netflix never crashes", "This diagram gets you hired", "Every app you use does this", "Interviewers ask this 90% of the time".
- Must work muted (half of views are muted).

## Format mix
- 2026 education-niche data: **carousels often outperform Reels for engagement in education** (up to 20 frames; swipe time counts as watch time; hybrid photo+3–5s video slides win). But Reels drive discovery/followers.
- Recommended cadence for growth accounts: 3–4 reels/week + 2–3 carousels/week. Given the 4 reels/week commitment and 5–8 hrs capacity: do 4 reels/week; recycle each reel's diagrams into 1–2 carousels/week (near-zero marginal cost).
- Trending audio can boost reach (~89% claim) — use instrumental trending audio under AI voiceover where it fits.

## Feasibility of 50k in 9 months (honest read)
- Benchmarks: faceless accounts posting 5×/week with Reels-first strategy typically hit **10k in 90–180 days**; 50k typically takes **6–12 months** with steady organic growth. Case studies exist of 0→15k in 30 days and 0→65k in 40 days, but those are outliers driven by 1–3 viral reels.
- Verdict: **50k in 9 months from zero is ambitious but within the plausible band** — it requires (a) never missing the 4/week cadence, (b) at least 2–4 breakout reels (>500k views) along the way, (c) ruthless iteration on hooks/retention data every 2 weeks.
- Growth will be back-loaded: expect ~1–3k by month 3, ~8–15k by month 6, then compounding. See success-metrics.md for pivot triggers.

## Account setup notes
- Niche-clear bio ("System design, explained simply — by a working engineer"), name field with searchable keywords ("Karthik | System Design"), highlight covers matching visual identity.
- Cross-post every reel to YouTube Shorts + TikTok + LinkedIn (free distribution, zero extra production).
